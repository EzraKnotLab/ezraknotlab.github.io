The audience put their attention mainly on 2 fights, namely co-main event and main event. The former one was between Justin 'Highlight' Gaethje and Rafael Fiziev. As we all know, Justin arguably is the most exciting performer to watch in this business nowadays, and his fighting style had earned him the most bonuses than anyone. His shows are always zealous for fans and newcomers to enjoy. Importantly, his opponent, Rafael Fiziev showed how nimble and technical he is in today's comain event. The newcomer of the lightweight division is tough and he can really take plenty of shots. To some extent, his speed may have something with his race, as Asian fighting out of Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan. The only pity for me is that his father told him if he win the game, the father would kill one sheep for the poor. I guess mainly the reason for that is Ramadan around the corner. Congrats to the brother and you can easily tell that next time, after some progress, he could get his hand raised in a title shot. No matter what comes, we just don't give up. Head up and come back ASAP.


Now on the main event, personally I can't see the influence of the headshot that the last time Leon landed on Kamaru left today. Leon showed his real level and tactics were carried out fabulously. More leg-kicks and excellent takedown defences made him retain the championship in the homecountry. The former champion didn't fight at that high level and his plans weren't selectively enough to help him get the belt back. Personally the coming fights I desired are Leon vs Colby, Kamaru vs the winner of the 'Belal vs Shavkat'. After the main fight, Kamaru said all the fighters went through a lot to be awfully prepared and perform for the fans. They definitely should get a reputation and respect. Shout out to heroes!
