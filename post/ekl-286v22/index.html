↩️

“我可以在这一刻欢欣鼓舞，下一刻却陷入沉思，而这一切的发生可能只是因为天上飘过了一朵云。”

那些凝视着地球之美的人发现了力量的储备……只要生命还在……在大自然的反复轮回中，有一种无限的治 愈力量——保证黑夜之后是黎明，冬天之后是春天。

所有生灵都俯视地面， 唯有人类被赋予了一张可以仰望星辰、凝视天空的脸。

你可能 还记得年轻的时候曾这么干过。那时，你有大把的时间可用，唯一的期限就是上床睡觉的时间。那时，你的想象力可以自由地飞。而一旦我们长大了，这种漫无目的的儿时乐趣会变得无聊起来。我们不再有时间无所事事地盯着天空、看云卷云舒。

观云是一种心境，而不是一项计划好的活动。

当天空上演一场表演秀时，你只需暂停手头正在做的事，并投入其中几分钟就可以了。

👑 Fighters

↩️

As half of a year in the making, the fight between Nigerian 'Nightmare' Kamaru Usman versus Leon 'Rocky' Edwards was held at The O2 arena in London, England. The audience put their attention mainly on 2 fights, namely co-main event and main event. The former one was between Justin 'Highlight' Gaethje and Rafael Fiziev. As we all know, Justin arguably is the most exciting performer to watch in this business nowadays, and his fighting style had earned him the most bonuses than anyone. His shows are always zealous for fans and newcomers to enjoy. Importantly, his opponent, Rafael Fiziev showed how nimble and technical he is in today's comain event. The newcomer of the lightweight division is tough and he can really take plenty of shots. To some extent, his speed may have something with his race, as Asian fighting out of Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan. The only pity for me is that his father told him if he win the game, the father would kill one sheep for the poor. I guess mainly the reason for that is Ramadan around the corner. Congrats to the brother and you can easily tell that next time, after some progress, he could get his hand raised in a title shot. No matter what comes, we just don't give up. Head up and come back ASAP.


Now on the main event, personally I can't see the influence of the headshot that the last time Leon landed on Kamaru left today. Leon showed his real level and tactics were carried out fabulously. More leg-kicks and excellent takedown defences made him retain the championship in the homecountry. The former champion didn't fight at that high level and his plans weren't selectively enough to help him get the belt back. Personally the coming fights I desired are Leon vs Colby, Kamaru vs the winner of the 'Belal vs Shavkat'. After the main fight, Kamaru said all the fighters went through a lot to be awfully prepared and perform for the fans. They definitely should get a reputation and respect. Shout out to heroes! They are fighters not only for the fans and sports but also for their family members, as a father, mother... Among the fighters, they got a lot of respect for each. Justin and Rafael showed their thoughts on that by tweeting 'It was an honour to share the cage'. Additionally, Justin is well educated by his parents and schools, and he acts and talks righteously at the same. This reminds me of the importance of knowledge and insights that humans have. Believably he has a love and wonderful family. There is something we can learn from outside the octagon. Almost forgot to say, Colby's fight is on the way. In the interview, he said he just doesn't like anyone else, who pictures the training and posts it on social media. He is bad in some ways, but you cannot deny that he's similar to big names like Leo Messi, and me. The elites do have something in common.

Life is f**king colorful for us to live in. We all fight together!

Till next , stay safe and stay hydrated!
