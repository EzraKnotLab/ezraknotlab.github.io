👑 Fighters cold😤

↩️

My first time watching fights at this new district: El 291 card was so wild. With DP&JG leading the main-event, we've all expected these kind of crazy scenes before. And that's exactly how it going.
KOPYLOV landed a head shot on RIBEIRD. Yeah, we can call the salt lake city as head-shot-dead city! HSDC!

FERGUSON and CHIESA were submitted by GREEN and HOLLAND individually. But the entrance song of Tony was still exciting and enjoyable, much attention and hit around the whole stadium. 'La Ladies and gentlemen, the party has just begun.'

LEWISM opened the first round with a upknee to DE LIMA, and destroyed the latter immediately, soon he finished the fight and leaving with the most KO record in the UFC history.

The co-main event was between Alex Pereira and JanBłachowicz. The cheering sound 'Let's go Alex' was extremely loud around the whole arena. The 1st round was awfully boring. After a hard-effort takedown, Jan controlled his opponent on the ground and totally made 2 submission attempts without much damage caused. During the 2nd and the last round, both on the feet tried to land more strikes, but Alex was the more active one, landed more shots on body and legs, even though Jan got takedowns and controlled him on the ground for some time. I'd been tremendously neverous since the second round. Cause I thought if Alex wants to win the fight without uncertainty, he needed to knock Jan out within 3 rounds. As time ticking, I was worried about how would the decision goes finally. In the end, Alex won by split decision, which makes sense for me. Like DC said right after the fight finished: 'I would say that Alex win the fight.' As a matter of fact, there're couple of takedowns got by Jan, who controlled his opponent for some time, but no too much effective damage made, instead his face was with blood. All in all, the fight was close though.

It's timmmmmmmeeeee!!!!!

  • The stage for the main event--BMF title was absolutely stunning and unique. I love these two fighters so much, either of them down would make my day awful. But let's fooking go then!

I don't know what's the name of Justin's walkout song, but I can't be more familiar with Dustin's 'The Boss'. It's just meant to be a dog fight...

The intensive first round looked normal at this most entertaining division--- lightweight. But still, two warriors landed some heavy kicks and jabs, the round was extremely close.
The second round was unforgettable in my life. Right after I post a pic on my ins' story for proving I was witnessing this historical moment in my life, 'The highlight' landed a huge head shot around Dustin's left shoulder. And 'The diamond' went down on the ground. My face was like this '😨' , it's so so surreal. I don't want this and I don't believe it.

And your BMF-

We're still young!
