☕Gooddays

↩️

  • 本来在看一篇LLNL的电池机理paper，感觉挺少见，因为电池领域通常是LANL（Los Alamos National Laboratory）的paper多，LANL跟奥本海默、曼哈顿计划挂钩，实验数据也多。这篇LLNL的偏理论计算，就去查了查实验室，发现LLNL是Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory，风格偏核物理、计算、理论。看到Livermore又想到元素周期表里有Livermorium（116号），一查发现是LLNL和俄罗斯Dubna的JINR合作打出来的。再往下看，115到118号元素全是他们联手，118号叫Oganesson，是纪念JINR的Yuri Oganessian。整个过程从电池机理看到超重元素合成，技术上都靠加速器、重离子束、分离器这些东西，突然觉得可能“从锂离子到超铀元素，科学从来不是孤岛，而是同一个加速器打出来的。”超重元素之父和门捷列夫都没Nobel Prize~

  • “These LLMs are what fire was for early human societies.”
  • AI ‘scientists’ joined these research teams: here’s what happened
    • Current co-scientist systems, like other LLM products, seem to be time-saving devices that can trigger insights by reminding users of things they had forgotten, kicking their brains into new modes, stating the obvious, flagging up background information they hadn’t previously seen or suggesting particular paths. Only time will tell how often this process throws up truly new and useful ideas.
      • To the future
      • For now, multi-agent LLM systems designed to help scientists with lab-meeting style reports are not readily available in easy-to-use formats. But Stevens expects that plenty of tech-savvy researchers and big tech firms will build systems with components aimed at different research needs, such as literature review or experiments. In May, the start-up firm FutureHouse in San Francisco, California, announced an autonomous AI discovery system, which it said combines a number of specialized AI modules to both review the literature and then suggest and design experiments (see go.nature.com/4df9gm8). Stevens expects that researchers will try giving co-scientists even more agency by coupling them to robotic systems in automated labs.
      • If young scientists take shortcuts by using LLMs, that could, for example, undermine their learning processes, says Stevens. “I feel we’re outsourcing a lot of our thought process,” says Barriga — including the fun part of coming up with ideas.

Islam Makhachev: 2-Champ Mode Activated

Boom – Islam just reclaimed the throne (Nov 15, UFC 322 at MSG). vacated lightweight to chase welterweight gold, then dominated Jack Della Maddalena via unanimous decision (50-45 x3) over 5 rounds. Smothering wrestling, calf kicks that had JDM limping, and tying Anderson Silva's UFC record with 16 straight wins. Two-division champ now (lightweight + welterweight), something even Khabib never grabbed. Dagestani eagle soaring – that's the ultimate "life's been good" moment in the cage. Post-fight, he's eyeing super-fights; this streak's unbreakable.
